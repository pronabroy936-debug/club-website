{{ profile.name }} presents the work of the organization as a social, educational, cultural, and sports institution. Visitors can understand the mission, see activities, check notifications, view media, and contact the team.

In 1955, under the leadership of Shanti Ghosh (Das)—the fiery daughter of the Agnijoog era, who, in 1931, assassinated Mr. Stevens, the District Magistrate of Comilla—the Vivekananda Sangathan was founded.

In 1958, under the presidentship of Shanti Das, the organization was registered under the Society Act. As a non-political organization, Vivekananda Sangathan has always been committed to shaping the sports and cultural landscape of the Kalyani subdivision.

In this refugee-dominated region, the organization has worked tirelessly to improve the quality of life of the people. It previously operated a crèche center and a free coaching center.

At present, it works year-round for underprivileged and meritorious students—distributing educational materials and helping them become self-reliant through computer training programs.

To encourage children to read and to provide unemployed job-seeking youth with a space to practice, the organization established the Nivedita Library.

For the mental and physical development of children affected by mobile addiction, Vivekananda Sports Academy was founded.

Football, athletics, chess, yoga, karate, and cricket are taught here by trained and experienced coaches. Students of the academy have begun achieving distinctions at both state and national levels.

Throughout the year, the organization inspires social awareness and national consciousness through blood-donation camps, tree-plantation drives, and national observance programs.

The organization also hosts the largest Vivekananda Festival in the Gayeshpur municipal area every year, featuring cultural programs, quiz competitions, debates, art competitions, and Nara-Narayan Seva.

This festival has now entered its 71st year. To inspire reading habits among children, the first Gayeshpur Book Fair is also being launched.
